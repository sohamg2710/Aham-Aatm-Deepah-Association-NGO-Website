Our Founder

Hi, I'm Harsh Makhija, the founder of Aham Aatm Deepah Association. My journey began with a personal mission to create a safe space for children and communities to heal and grow. Through education, emotional support, and mindfulness, I aim to empower individuals to overcome challenges and embrace their inner light. Healing through Service, Spirituality, and Sharing

A Jaipur-based NGO uplifting children and communities through emotional well-being, education, and volunteer-led healing. We have come a long way of serving over 3000 children and families in slum areas, providing them with essential resources and emotional support. And we didn't stop just there! We started the Shiksha Project, a meditation-based academic and spiritual education program that has transformed the lives of many children and families.

Our Projects

Shiksha Project

Started in July 2024, we took on the mission to educate and sensitize young minds to be emotionally well and academically sharp. We started the shiksha project focused upon personalized teaching, practicing super brain yoga and other stimulating activities to keep them curious and ended up with an average increase of 7 percent in their annual results.

Monthly Resource Allocation

Living in the slums, fighting extreme weather, hunger and negativity it gets tough to survive and we understand that. And thus we distribute excess resources lying around your homes to help these souls in need. We collect clothes, dry ration and other necessary resources directly from your homes, store them and distribute them with our volunteers.

The Healing Society Project

A safe space to vent out and let go of those emotions, focused on teenagers who are looking for a place to rest, heal, and let go. We hear you. Share your story in your own private space.
